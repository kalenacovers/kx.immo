# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners in Baden-Württemberg who have already decided to sell. They arrive with their biggest financial decision already made; they are evaluating whether Julia Braun is the right person to trust with it. They are not comparing prices or browsing options: they want confirmation of competence and reliability. Age range typically 40-70. German-speaking. High expectations for professionalism; low tolerance for noise, sales pressure, or amateur presentation.

## Product Purpose

KX Immobilien is Julia Braun's real estate brokerage, specializing in discrete property sales, premium marketing, and property management in Baden-Württemberg. The site's single job is to convert visiting homeowners into first-contact leads: a form submission, a phone call, or an email. Success looks like a visitor who arrived uncertain and leaves confident enough to pick up the phone.

## Brand Personality

Precise, modern, efficient. The brand speaks with the quiet authority of someone who has done this many times and doesn't need to prove it loudly. Confidence without arrogance. Professionalism without coldness.

## Anti-references

- **Generic real estate portals** (ImmobilienScout24, Zillow): database-heavy, blue/white, transactional, commodity feel. KX Immobilien is the opposite of a portal.
- **Corporate luxury brands**: Swiss-bank cold, distanced, untouchable. Premium without warmth is alienating for homeowners making a personal decision.
- **SaaS landing pages**: gradient heroes, floating metric cards, social-proof grids, startup energy. This is a trusted local expert, not a growth-hacking product.
- **Regional small-business sites**: cluttered, low-contrast, amateur feel. Julia's positioning is the direct opposite.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust through restraint.** Precision means no visual noise. Every element earns its place by building confidence, not by filling space. Remove rather than add.
2. **The seller's moment.** Visitors arrive decided. The design matches that certainty: no hesitation, no persuasion theatre, no hedging. Direct, composed, unhurried.
3. **Architectural hierarchy.** Spacing, type scale, and structure work like a well-built room: nothing is arbitrary, everything serves a purpose, the hierarchy reads immediately.
4. **Local credential, global standard.** Regional presence is a strength, not a limitation. The site looks world-class while anchoring itself in Baden-Württemberg's character.
5. **Efficiency signals competence.** A clean, fast, direct experience is itself the brand message: Julia doesn't waste time, and neither does the site.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AAA target. Higher contrast ratios beyond AA minimum. Existing features (reduced-motion support, focus-visible rings, skip link, ARIA labels) are the floor, not the ceiling. Color choices must pass AAA contrast where practical. Form inputs must be fully navigable by keyboard.
